Broken Chord

Game Development I Project, 2021

Engine: Godot

Language: GDScript

Role: Developer, Programmer

Broken Chord is a proof of concept for a rhythm-based dungeon crawler developed using the Godot Engine where the player must move and cast spells to the beat to overcome the trials of the dungeon.

Some of my contributions to Broken Chord include:
  • Developing a simple AI used by the enemies for navigation and combat
  • Working with the team's other programmer to implement player controls and movement
