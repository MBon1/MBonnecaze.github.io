Racing Game using Microphone and Wii Balance Board Inputs

Experimental Game Design Project, 2023

Engine: Unity

Language: C#

Itch Page

GitHub

This proof of concept explores unconventional controls for a racing game, where the player controls a vehicle (a surfboard) using a microphone and a Wii Balance Board.
Movement is controlled through the microphone, with the volume of the audio input determining the force applied to the surfboard. The louder the sound, the greater the force.
Steering is achieved by leaning on the Wii Balance Board, which is positioned sideways to replicate the stance of riding a real board.

Created in response to the prompt "Weird Input," this project highlights how alternative hardware can create unique and engaging gaming experiences.


A gameplay demonstration showcasing the surfboard being controlled through audio volume, visualized by a volume meter at the top left of the UI, and body movement, represented by a directional visualizer at the bottom left.
